Research

I am interested in the local side of the langlands program esecially on how properties of algebraic objects affect the analytic properties of representaions on them. I am currently working on the gan gross prasad conjecture for the general spin group. A current draft of my thesis is below which contains a draft of the multiplicity one conjecture in the special orthogonal case and will eventually contain a local trace formula.

Previosly I focused more on the analytic side of things and studyed modular forms. In my undergraduate thesis I studied Siegel Eisenstein Series. Ichino has shown that certain values of some symmetric square L-functions can be expressed as the inner product of a Saito-Kurokawa lift. It is also know that there is a pullback formula which expresses Saito-Kurokawa lifsts in terms of genus one cusp forms. We can then take advantage of the finite dimensionality of the vector space of cusp forms to calculate our desired value with simple linear algebra.
